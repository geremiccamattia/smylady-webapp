2. PURPOSE OF THE APP

Share Your Party is an event discovery and ticketing app that allows users aged 13 and above to:

  • Discover events in their area
  • Buy and sell tickets for events
  • Share events with friends
  • Build a community of event attendees and organizers

3. DATA PROCESSED BY THE APP

We process various types of data to provide you with the best possible app experience.

3.1 ACCOUNT DATA (UPON REGISTRATION)

Mandatory information upon registration:

  • Username – For identification in the app
  • Email address – For account management and important notifications
  • Password – Stored encrypted, never in plain text
  • Date of birth – For age verification (minimum age 13 years)

Optional information:

  • Interests – Music, Gastronomy, Business, Sports, Outdoor, On the Roof, Theme, Boat, Other (for personalized event recommendations)
  • Profile picture – If you want to upload a photo
  • Description – Optional self-description
  • Location – Automatically captured if you grant location permission

Legal basis:

  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR (contract fulfillment) for mandatory information
  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent) for optional information

3.2 SOCIAL LOGIN DATA

If you sign in via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we receive the following data from the respective provider:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Registration date
  • Unique user ID of the provider

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent)

3.3 EVENT AND TICKET DATA

Regarding events, we receive the following data:

  • Attended events
  • Purchased tickets (ticket number, price, event details)
  • Sold tickets (if you are an event organizer)
  • Event favorites and saved events
  • Check-in data at events (if you use our check-in)

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR (contract fulfillment)

3.4 PAYMENT INFORMATION

When purchasing event tickets through our app, payments are processed via Stripe. The following data is processed:

  • Credit card or payment data (transmitted directly to Stripe)
  • Transaction history (date, amount, event)
  • Billing address

IMPORTANT: Complete credit card data is NOT stored on our servers, but exclusively processed encrypted via Stripe. We only receive an anonymized transaction ID.

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR (contract fulfillment)

Stripe Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

3.5 LOCATION DATA

If you grant location permission, we collect:

  • Current location (GPS coordinates) – To show events near you
  • City/Region – For event recommendations

Usage:

  • Display of events in your area
  • Personalized event recommendations based on your location
  • Map display of event locations

Control: You can choose in your profile settings whether your location is displayed to other users.

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent)

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.

3.6 DEVICE INFORMATION

We automatically collect the following technical data:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version (Android/iOS)
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Language and time zone
  • Mobile network information

Purpose: Technical operation, error diagnosis, security

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest)

3.7 USAGE BEHAVIOR

We collect the following data when using the app:

  • App usage statistics (which features are used)
  • Clicks and interactions within the app
  • Search histories and filter preferences
  • Session duration and frequency of app usage
  • Crash reports and performance data

Purpose: App improvement, personalization, error analysis

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent via cookie banner)

3.8 COMMUNICATION DATA

  • Email communication with us
  • Support requests via the contact form
  • Feedback and reviews

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest) or Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR

3.9 SOCIAL MEDIA FEATURES & POSTS

Share Your Party offers you the opportunity to create posts about events and share them with other users. The following data is processed:

Post content:

  • Texts and descriptions – Your written content
  • Images and photos – Visual content uploaded by you
  • Timestamp – When the post was created
  • Event link – Which event the post belongs to
  • Author information – Your username and profile picture
  • Hashtags and mentions – If you tag other users (@username) or use hashtags

Visibility settings:

You can choose for each post who can see it:

  • Only for me – Only you can see the post (private)
  • Only for subscribers – Only users who follow you can see the post
  • Public – All Share Your Party users can see the post

Interaction data:

  • Likes/Reactions – Who liked your post
  • Comments – Comments on your posts (with username and timestamp)
  • Shares – How often your post was shared
  • Views – How many users viewed your post
  • Follower/Subscriber list – Who follows you and whom you follow

Feed and recommendations:

We use an algorithm to show you relevant posts in the feed. We consider:

  • Your interests and past interactions
  • Events you favorited or attended
  • Posts from users you follow
  • Popular and trending content

Deletion of posts:

You can delete your posts at any time in the app.

IMPORTANT: Deleted posts will no longer be visible to other users, but remain stored in our database for a certain period:

  • For you: Deleted posts are immediately invisible
  • In the database: Posts are marked as "deleted" for 30 days (for moderation purposes and to prevent abuse)
  • Final deletion: After 30 days, posts and associated images are permanently deleted

Reporting inappropriate content:

Users can report posts that violate our community guidelines. Upon report:

  • The post is reviewed by our moderation team
  • We store the report with timestamp and reporting user
  • The post may be removed if it violates our guidelines
  • Repeated violations may result in account suspension

Legal basis:

  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR (contract fulfillment) for post creation and management
  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest) for feed algorithm and moderation
  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent) for public posts and sharing on social media

3.10 EVENT CREATION FOR ORGANIZERS

As an event organizer, you can create and manage your own events in the Share Your Party app. The following data is processed:

Mandatory information for event creation:

  • Date & Time – When the event takes place
  • Location/Venue – Address or location of the event (with GPS coordinates for map display)
  • Number of guests – Maximum number of participants
  • Event type – Type of event
  • Event category – Event category (e.g., Music, Gastronomy, Business, Sports, Outdoor, On the Roof, Theme, Boat, Other)
  • Music type – What music will be played
  • Additional offers – Extras such as pool, drinks & food, etc.
  • Event name – Title of the event
  • Description – Detailed description of the event
  • Restrictions – Special rules or requirements (e.g., dress code)
  • Minimum age – Age restriction for the event
  • Event photo – Cover image for the event

Visibility settings for events:

As an organizer, you can choose who can see your event:

  • Public – All Share Your Party users can find the event and buy tickets
  • Only for subscribers – Only users who follow you can see the event
  • Only for specific friends – You select specific users who can see the event (private events)

Additional data:

  • Organizer profile – Your username and profile picture are linked to the event
  • Ticket information – If you sell tickets: price, availability, ticket categories
  • Participant list – Who bought tickets (visible to you as organizer)
  • Check-in status – Who attended the event
  • Event statistics – Views, favorites, shared events

Deletion of events:

You can delete your events under certain conditions:

  • Deletion possible: As long as NO tickets have been sold, you can delete the event at any time
  • Deletion NOT possible: Once at least one ticket has been sold, the event can no longer be deleted (protection of ticket buyers)

Upon event deletion, the following is deleted:

  • All event details and descriptions
  • Event photos
  • Event statistics
  • Links to categories and tags

Event moderation:

Events can be reported by other users if they violate our community guidelines. Upon report:

  • The event is reviewed by our moderation team
  • The event may be removed if it violates our guidelines (e.g., fake events, inappropriate content)
  • Repeated violations may result in suspension of your organizer account

What data do ticket buyers see?

When someone buys a ticket for your event, you as organizer receive:

  • Buyer's username
  • Number of purchased tickets
  • Purchase date
  • Check-in status at the event

NOT transmitted: Email address, date of birth, full profile data (unless the buyer makes their profile publicly visible)

Legal basis:

  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR (contract fulfillment) for event creation and ticket management
  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest) for event moderation and statistics
  • Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent) for public events

6. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

6.1 STORAGE LOCATION

Your data is stored on secure servers:

  • Server location: Amazon Web Services (AWS) – Region eu-central-1 (Frankfurt, Germany)
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Backup locations: Within the European Union

IMPORTANT: Your main data is stored and processed exclusively in the EU. When using some third-party services (e.g., Firebase, Stripe, SendGrid), data may be transferred to the USA. These transfers are based on EU standard contractual clauses according to Art. 46 GDPR.

6.2 SECURITY MEASURES

We protect your data through comprehensive security measures:

  • Encrypted transmission: All data is transmitted encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted storage: Passwords are hashed; sensitive data is stored encrypted
  • Access controls: Only authorized employees have access to user data (principle of least privilege)
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection: Our servers are protected by modern security systems
  • Regular security updates: All systems are updated promptly
  • Secure payment processing: Payment data is processed exclusively via PCI-DSS certified partners (Stripe)

6.3 STORAGE DURATION

We store your data only as long as necessary for the respective purposes.

Data Type Storage Duration
Account data Until account deletion
Event and ticket data Until account deletion (transaction data see below)
Payment and invoice data 10 years (legal retention obligation under Austrian tax law)
Usage statistics (anonymized) 26 months (Google Analytics standard)
Server logfiles 7 days
Support requests 3 years after completion
Cookies According to cookie settings (max. 13 months)

8.6 RIGHT TO OBJECT (ART. 21 GDPR)

Upon account deletion, the following data will be deleted:

  • Your event preferences and interests
  • Your saved events and favorites

This data is stored in such a way that it can no longer be associated with you as a person.
